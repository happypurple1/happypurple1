### About Me

I'm a mathematician with an M.S. in Applied Mathematics, currently pursuing a second master's in Mechanical Engineering. I work across math education, AI, and engineering.

**AI & Data** — I work with [Outlier](https://outlier.ai) training large language models, writing mathematical "stumpers" to stress-test model reasoning, evaluating response accuracy, and reviewing image recognition outputs.

**Teaching** — 11+ years of math instruction spanning college-level Calculus, Trigonometry, and Precalculus through secondary Algebra 2 (on-level and advanced, TEKS-aligned). I currently teach at Amarillo High School and tutor Differential Equations. I build curriculum from scratch in LaTeX and automate data workflows in Python.

**Engineering** — Graduate coursework in mechanical engineering with research involving Cross-Impact Analysis methodology.
