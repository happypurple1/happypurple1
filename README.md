### About Me

I teach Algebra 2 (on-level and advanced) at Amarillo High School and I'm pursuing my Master's in Mechanical Engineering. I build rigorous, TEKS-aligned curriculum from scratch and I'm interested in how data analytics and engineering thinking connect to math education.

I make most of my materials in LaTeX, automate data workflows in Python, and use tools like DeltaMath, Desmos, and Canvas to deliver instruction.
